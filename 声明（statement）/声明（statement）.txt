It is hereby stated that from now on, the code date on GitHub every day will not use the common era chronology, but the ancient Chinese Taoist calendar chronology.
For example: May 7, 2023, that is, March 18 of the 4720 lunar calendar. I respect the culture of each country, and I also hope that the people of the world can respect the excellent culture of our Huaxia 5000.
